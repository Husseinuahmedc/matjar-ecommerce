# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Matjar serves Iraqi buyers, sellers, admins, and support staff. Buyers browse bilingual product catalogs, manage carts, place orders, and track purchases. Sellers manage inventory, approvals, and order fulfillment. Admin and support users monitor users, orders, catalog health, and operational issues.

## Product Purpose

Matjar is a bilingual Arabic/English multi-seller e-commerce platform for the Iraqi market using IQD pricing, Supabase authentication, Prisma-backed commerce data, and role-specific dashboards. Success means each role can complete its core workflow with real data, clear feedback, safe mutations, and responsive interfaces.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, practical, and polished. The interface should feel like a serious commerce operations product: familiar enough for daily use, refined enough to inspire confidence, and direct in Arabic and English copy.

## Anti-references

Avoid placeholder storefronts, university-project dashboards, decorative SaaS clichés, hardcoded demo data, inconsistent controls, weak empty states, and visual patterns that compete with operational clarity.

## Design Principles

Use real operational context everywhere.
Make every role-specific workflow obvious from the first screen.
Prefer familiar commerce patterns over novelty.
Keep bilingual and RTL/LTR behavior first-class.
Show clear state for loading, empty, error, success, and disabled interactions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Preserve keyboard focus rings, readable contrast, responsive layouts at mobile widths, and reduced-motion-safe interactions. Arabic RTL and English LTR layouts must remain equally usable.
